# Changelog

## v0.3.0

Released May 12, 2024.

- Fixed drawings not appearing depending on the script execution order.
- Adds documentation comments to ShapeDrawer.

Released May 10, 2024.

## v0.1.0

Released May 9, 2024.

- Adds line and wireframe drawing.
